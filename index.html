Privacy Policy

App: Apex Odds: Bet Tracker
Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

Apex Odds ("the App") is a personal betting tracker. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Data you enter

All betting records you create — selections, odds, stakes, results, notes, bankrolls, bookmakers and tipsters — are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Subscription & purchase data

If you subscribe to Apex Odds Premium, your purchase is processed entirely by Google Play. We receive only an anonymised confirmation that a valid subscription exists. We do not receive or store your payment card details, name, or billing address.

Analytics & crash data

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage data of any kind. No tracking SDKs are included in the App.

3. Data Storage

All your betting data is stored in your device's local storage using Android's SharedPreferences system. It is not backed up to any cloud service by the App itself. If you uninstall the App, your data is deleted from your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Play Billing to process subscription payments. Google's privacy policy applies to that transaction: policies.google.com/privacy

The App uses Android local notifications to send optional result reminders. No notification data leaves your device.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

6. Your Rights

Because we store no personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete or export on your behalf. To erase your betting data, simply uninstall the App.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
